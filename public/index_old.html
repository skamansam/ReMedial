ReMedial

Any media. Anywhere.

About your application’s environment

ReMedial is a web-based program used to manage your media. It can handle photos (html slideshow), music(flash player) and videos (flash player) straight from your browser. It can also create playlists for use in external players. As a design philosophy, I am keeping the database very small and using the filesystem for playlists and upload.
